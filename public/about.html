We are the Passion Fruits

a non-profit web & software development company




We are a small company located in BC, Canada

Our goal is to make the world a little more fun. Whether it be by making games, sharing software, or even teaching people how to code, we want people to be able to express themselves as creatively as possible.

We believe that technology should be 100% free and accessible to anyone, regardless of your location, living conditions, or ethnicity.

We are fueled by a passion for making things and a love for bringing people joy. Join us, and maybe you'll be able to spread some coolness too!
